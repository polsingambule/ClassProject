🌑 Scope of Humanities After 12th

👉 Be it honing and facilitating the essential skills to appear in the most competitive government exams, or instilling professional job-oriented skills, humanities courses offer a wide spectrum of opportunities. From job roles like editor, multimedia specialist, digital marketing specialist, archivist, to roles like analyst, psychologist, data linguist, there are a varied array of job options available for students after completing their 12th in arts.

👉 Apart from B.A. courses, students can also enroll for integrated courses giving them an upper hand in the job market. To get admission in an Integrated course, an aspirant must have scored 50% in the qualifying exam.
